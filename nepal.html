How to record your Volunteer Time:

  1. Log onto IBM Benevity (https://ibm.benevity.org/) by using your IBM W3 ID
  2. Under "Volunteer", select "Track Volunteer time".
  3. Select "Something Else", and type what you volunteered (use this method for CTF Nepal) for OR
  4. Select the "Specific Cause" you volunteered in, and enter the Cause Name
  5. Note down the number of hours and the date you volunteered for
  6. Choose "Donation Currency"
  7. Write briefly about what you did when volunteering
  8. Submit the form by clicking "Confirm Volunteer Time"

How to Donate your Volunteer Rewards:

  1. Log onto IBM Benevity (https://ibm.benevity.org/) by using your IBM W3 ID
  2. On your Dashboard, search for our charity partner "Engage Nepal with Science"
  3. Click on the blue "Donate Now" button below the heading
  4. Choose "Onetime" for Donation Frequency
  5. Submit the donation to the charity by clicking "Confirm Donation"

Ideally, the donations should be sent by the 3rd of March as it would need a few weeks to get processed, and payments to charities begin on the 21st of the month following your donation's processed date.
